Curriculum

Maintenance Operations

Covers machine operation, maintenance, troubleshooting, and safety procedures, leading to an OSHA 10-hour course completion card.

Basic Mechanical Systems

Teaches mechanical power transmission safety, installation, alignment, lubrication, and troubleshooting of chains, belts, bearings, and gear trains.

Basic Hydraulic Systems

Introduces hydraulic system principles, components, safety, and troubleshooting, focusing on fluid power schematics and regulations.

Basic Pneumatic Systems

Explains pneumatic principles, including air compressors, valves, cylinders, motors, and safety regulations in fluid power systems.

Electrical Systems

Introduces AC/DC electrical fundamentals, motor control circuits, schematics, wiring diagrams, and electrical components like relays and switches.
